# Risk And Service-Level Catalog

Common metrics and objectives:

- expected cost or reward,
- expected utility,
- variance or standard deviation penalty,
- CVaR,
- VaR,
- mean-CVaR weighted objective,
- chance constraint violation probability,
- service level,
- fill rate,
- worst-case objective,
- regret and maximum regret,
- distributionally robust expected cost.

Document:

- confidence level or risk level,
- objective sense,
- whether the risk measure is coherent or convex,
- sample estimator,
- in-sample and out-of-sample reporting method,
- business interpretation.
